# Star Ventures Sales Dashboard

## Overview

The Star Ventures Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze sales performance across customer segments, countries, and time periods. The dashboard provides stakeholders with a clear view of key business metrics, helping identify trends, monitor profitability, and support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Problem

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, but turning that data into actionable insights can be challenging. This dashboard was developed to provide a centralized view of sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and geographic trends, enabling decision-makers to quickly evaluate business performance.

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Gross Sales
* Total Discounts
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin (%)

### Interactive Analysis

* Year-based filtering
* Country-based filtering
* Sales trend analysis over time
* Segment performance analysis
* Geographic sales comparison

### Visualizations

* Sales by Customer Segment
* Sales by Country
* Monthly Gross Sales Trend
* KPI Summary Cards

## Key Insights

* Total Gross Sales reached **$127.93M**.
* Total Profit amounted to **$16.89M**.
* Profit Margin was **13.21%**.
* Government customers generated the largest share of sales (**44.22%**).
* Small Business customers contributed **35.74%** of total sales.
* The United States and Canada were the strongest-performing markets.
* Sales activity fluctuated throughout the reporting period, with several notable peaks in 2014.
